//WHO WE ARE

CAUTION TAPE ROBOTCS, TEAM 839

At Caution Tape Robotics VRC Team 839, we are an agile group of like minded students in middle school and high school who have a passion for STEM, competition, and robotics. VRC Team 839 has one middle school team, 839Z, and one high school team, 839A. Combined, we have over 12 passionate team members all contributing to different parts of the competition. We also have three coaches, Daniel, James, and Malhar. Our team pushes for high results and gets creative for all the challenges presented in the VEX Robotics Competition.

THE ROBOTS

TEAM 839A

VRC Team 839A is a High School VEX Robotics Competition team. Team 839A competes with the top teams in Ontario, having won one Tournament Champion award as well as multiple Tournament Finalist awards. The team members on 839A have gained experience in VRC throughout their three seasons currently, and many members have past robotics experiences from competitions such as FLL and WRO. 839A also helps guide the way for 839Z in their journey in VEX.

TEAM 839Z

VRC Team 839Z is a Middle School VEX Robotics Competition team. Team 839Z is comprised of many hard-working students in the middle school age group, working together to achieve greatness. 839Z has put out many impressive results, such as Venus Division Skills Champion at the 2021 VEX World Championships. Team 839Z is also a place where team members can develop their skills in VEX, to be ready when the day to move up to 839A comes.
