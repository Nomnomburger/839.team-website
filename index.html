CHANGE UP

VEX Robotics Competition Change Up is played on a 12’x12’ square field configured as seen above. Two Alliances – one “red” and one “blue” – composed of two Teams each, compete in matches consisting of a fifteen second Autonomous Period, followed by a one minute and forty-five second (1:45) Driver Controlled Period. The object of the game is to attain a higher score than the opposing Alliance by placing Balls in Goals, and Connecting Rows.

INAUGURAL CAUTION TAPE CLASSIC

Welcome to the first every Caution Tape Classic! This special event was created to allow teams, during the global Covid-19 pandemic, to still participate in live skills matches in a safe and managed way. A limited number of teams were allowed, and the teams were also subject to VEX judging, something not previously possible with online remote competitions. Special custom trophies were given out to celebrate this special event!
