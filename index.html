ABOUT OUR CLUB

The 2021 VRC World Championship was a great way to end off the season. Middle school team 839Z scored a combined point of 198 in the second (Venus) division, taking home division skills champion as well as Think Award. High school team 839A was placed in the strong first (Mercury) division, but unfortunately due to autonomous run issues only scored 198 points. Overall, the team had a lot of fun preparing and attending remote VEX Worlds!
