CAUTION TAPE CLASSIC

Welcome to the first every Caution Tape Classic! This special event was created to allow teams, during the global Covid-19 pandemic, to still participate in live skills matches in a safe and managed way. A limited number of teams were allowed, and the teams were also subject to VEX judging, something not previously possible with online remote competitions. Special custom trophies were given out to celebrate this special event!
